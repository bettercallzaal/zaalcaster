What is Zaalcaster?

Zaalcaster is Zaal's personal Farcaster client - a lightweight, open-source reader and posting tool for anyone who wants a clean, purpose-built interface to Farcaster. Everyone can read; only Zaal posts.

It's a daily cockpit for managing replies, monitoring channels, drafting posts in a personal voice, and engaging your community - all without the noise of a general social feed.

Core Features

Daily Cockpit

Unanswered inbound + top-of-feed in one screen. See what needs a reply before anything else.

Reply Queue

Ranked by sender credibility (neynar score, follows, mutual connections) - the highest-signal replies first.

Channel Feeds

Read specific channels (/zao, /wavewarz, /zabal) without the main feed noise.

Reply Drafts

AI-powered draft suggestions grounded in ZAO context and your personal voice. Never posts without your approval.

Open Source

MIT licensed. Fork it. Run your own. No hidden costs. Pure Neynar API - one signer, one key.

Zero Dependencies

Node 20+ built-ins only. No npm packages. Deploy anywhere.

Introducing $zaalcaster

A token for attention and alignment

$zaalcaster is more than a token - it's a mechanism for signaling what you think deserves Zaal's reply attention, with skin in the game.

How It Works

Two levers, both you control:

Hold - Stake

Show how much $zaalcaster you believe in by staking it. Your standing balance is visible when your replies queue up. Signals long-term alignment.

Spend - Tip

Attach a tip to a specific Farcaster link you want Zaal to see. 50% burned (deflationary), 50% to Zaal. Pay-per-boost for the posts that matter most to you.

The Flow: Open zaalcaster → submit a Farcaster link + tip amount → enters Zaal's reply queue, ranked by (your stake + tip) → Zaal's judgment applies (human stays in the loop) → he replies

Economics

  • Zaal keeps ~50%, vested over month one. No dump - signals alignment.
  • Contributor rewards are discretionary - valuable PRs earn tokens at Zaal's judgment.
  • Anti-spam floor on tips prevents dust submissions.
  • Zaal's judgment is the final ranker - a big holder + small tip vs small holder + big tip is his call. Prevents pay-to-spam.

Why It Works

Three demand sources converge: hold (stake), spend (tips), and scarcity (burn). Revenue to Zaal on every boost. Skin-in-the-game from everyone who tips. No false promises - Zaal replies on his judgment, which keeps it real.

Quick Start

For Readers

Clone the repo. Install Node 20+. Set your Neynar API key. Read the timeline, channels, or search. Free. Open source. No accounts.

For Posters

Same setup + mint a Neynar managed signer (server-side, your key never leaves Neynar). Now you can post, reply, and participate.

For Builders

Fork it as <yourusername>caster. Make it yours. Contribute valuable features back - Zaalcaster rewards PRs with $zaalcaster tokens.
