# Zaalcaster One-Pager

## What Is It

Zaalcaster is Zaal's personal Farcaster client - a lightweight, open-source reader and posting tool. Everyone can read; only Zaal posts. It's his daily cockpit for managing replies, monitoring channels, and engaging his community without the noise.

**Built on:** Node 20+, Neynar v2, open-source MIT license.

## Core Experience

- **Daily Cockpit** - Unanswered inbound + top-of-timeline in one screen. See what needs a reply first.
- **Reply Queue** - Ranked by sender credibility (Neynar score, follows, mutual connections). Highest-signal first.
- **Channel Feeds** - /zao, /wavewarz, /zabal without main-feed noise.
- **AI Reply Drafts** - Suggestions grounded in ZAO context. Never posts without Zaal's approval.
- **Zero Dependencies** - Node built-ins only. Deploy anywhere.
- **Open Source** - MIT. Fork as your own username-caster.

## Introducing $zaalcaster Token

A mechanism for signaling what deserves Zaal's reply attention, with skin in the game.

### Two Levers

1. **Hold - Stake** - Hold $zaalcaster to show alignment. Your balance is visible in the reply queue.
2. **Spend - Tip** - Attach a tip to a specific Farcaster link. 50% burned (deflationary), 50% to Zaal.

### The Mechanism

Submit link + tip → enters Zaal's reply queue, ranked by (your stake + tip) → Zaal's judgment applies (human stays in the loop) → he replies.

### Economics

- **Zaal keeps ~50%**, vested over month one (no dump - signals alignment).
- **Contributors earn tokens** at Zaal's judgment for valuable PRs.
- **Anti-spam floor** on tips.
- **Zaal's judgment is final ranker** - stake + tip are inputs, not automation. Prevents pay-to-spam.

### Why It Works

Three demand sources converge: hold (stake), spend (tips), scarcity (burn). Revenue to Zaal on every boost. Skin-in-the-game from everyone. No false promises - Zaal's judgment keeps it real.
